Advanced security driven by local expertise.

Safe Security Systems was founded in Hermanus and has grown to become one of the largest local alarm monitoring and armed response service providers in the Western Cape.

The company currently serves the wider Overstrand region and expanded into the Cape Peninsula through an office in Somerset West. Headquartered in Hermanus, the control room provides alarm monitoring and customer support services. Safe Security Systems prides itself on keeping pace with technological advances in security equipment and delivering a level of service that larger security firms often cannot match.

Today, Safe Security Systems monitors home, government, and business security systems for more than 7,500 customers.
